How we came to beAbout Commune Café

Founded by Rosario Juan, a passionate coffee enthusiast and social media strategist deeply involved in the Philippine coffee industry, Commune is a cafe born from the desire to combine a love for coffee with fostering meaningful conversations. Rosario's dream of opening a cafe materialized after her extensive engagement with the Philippine Coffee Board, Inc. (PCBI) and managing a cafe in Shanghai for four years. Returning to Manila as a full-time social media strategist, she recognized the importance of offline connections, organizing events and meetups. This experience fueled the realization of the need for a dedicated venue, leading to the establishment of Commune.

Commune is not just a café; it's a dynamic community hub fostering connections and meaningful conversations. Positioned as an escape from urban hustle, it offers patrons a space to unwind with comfort food and high-quality Philippine coffees, sourced through collaborations with the Philippine Coffee Board, Inc. (PCBI). Founder Rosario Juan envisions Commune as a champion for Philippine coffee, emphasizing community collaboration and acting as a catalyst for diverse communities. Located in Makati City, Commune aims to be a space where great coffee and connections come together.
